Foundation

Why Spacing Matters

Padding isn't just about looking "nice." It does real structural work on every page.

01
Guide the Eye

Spacing directs where the viewer looks next. Consistent rhythm creates a natural reading path from headline to CTA.

02
Separate & Group

Proximity signals relationship. Tight spacing groups content; generous gaps signal new sections without needing borders or backgrounds.

03
Breathable Elegance

Luxury is communicated through what's left out. Generous white space is the single strongest signal that a site is premium, not template-y.

Apply

Apply to Your Drafts

Once you've reviewed the principles, run each draft through this three-step spacing audit.

01
Section Blocks

Review margins and paddings on every section block. Identify your base spacing value and audit every section against it.

  • Are all similar sections using the same vertical padding?
  • Is there at least one breathing gap between each content block?
02
Header vs. Paragraph

Compare the spacing between headline, subhead, body copy, and CTA. Each relationship should have a deliberate, consistent gap.

  • Is there more space above a heading than below it?
  • Does the CTA have enough room to breathe as a standalone action?
03
Mobile vs. Desktop

Check spacing behavior at multiple breakpoints. Padding that works at 1440px often becomes cramped or excessive at 375px.

  • Do section paddings scale proportionally on mobile?
  • Are text line lengths constrained at all viewport sizes?
