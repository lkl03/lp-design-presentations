Your Checklist

Mobile QA Checklist

Run every item on this list at 375px before submitting for review.

01
Navigation

Confirm hamburger renders correctly and all menu items are reachable via tap.

  • Nav collapses to hamburger at mobile
  • No overflow or overlap in nav bar
  • Menu opens and closes without bugs
02
Layout & Overflow

Scroll the entire page at 375px. Confirm no horizontal scroll at any point.

  • No horizontal scroll anywhere on the page
  • Split blocks restack: image above, text below
  • CTAs are large enough to tap comfortably
03
Imagery & Heroes

Check all hero images and full-bleed sections at 375px. Focal content must remain visible.

  • Faces and key features not cropped on mobile
  • Overlay on text-over-image heroes is legible
  • No blurry or stretched images at any size
