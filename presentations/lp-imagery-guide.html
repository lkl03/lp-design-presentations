Your Checklist

Imagery QA Checklist

Run every item before submitting a site for review.

01
Quality & Licensing

Verify every image is high-resolution, professionally shot, and fully licensed for use.

  • No low-res, pixelated, or stretched images
  • No watermarks on any image
  • Source is client-provided, Dash, or licensed stock
02
Crops & Overlays

Confirm hero images crop correctly at all breakpoints and all text-over-image areas have a proper overlay.

  • Faces & features visible at 375px, 768px, 1440px
  • All text-on-image areas have gradient or overlay
  • No text lost against a busy background
03
Consistency & Speed

Confirm image treatment is uniform across sections and all files are optimized for web delivery.

  • Headshots share the same crop ratio
  • Property images consistent aspect ratio per section
  • Images optimized â€” heroes under 300KB
